Context

Circuit of the Americas is a 3.4 mile race track and facilities located in Austin, Texas. It is the only FIA Grade 1 Formula 1 track in the United States. When they aren't hosting Formula 1, MotoGP, NASCAR, the venue is home to a variety of private auto manufacturing programs, driving experiences, and other live events.

The Audi Driving Experience aims to inspire potential customers to immerse themselves in the world of Audi, inviting them to discover the thrill of driving excellence firsthand. ADE consists of 4 different programs at varying levels of difficulty, each with its own unique driving experience. It is the duty of the sales representatives to ensure customers are on track to succeed.

Mission

The goal of this project is to produce compelling sales collateral for the Audi Driving Experience at Circuit of The Americas. The design should focus on showcasing the thrill, luxury, and innovation synonymous with the Audi brand, while supplying the customer with the information they need.

The aim of this collateral is to highlight each driving program and it's features, enticing potential participants to book their own exhilarating experience.

The official font to be used is Audi Type. As for the overarching appearance of Audi branding, adjacent font styles are available. It is set in red in particularly brand building areas. Descriptive copy, technical details and long passages of copy appear in black or white.
The Audi Sport brand colours are: Audi Sport Red, Audi Sport Dark Red, White, Black and Audi Aluminium Silver.

Brand colours are always used as solid colours.

Shades of grey can be used to support the colour spectrum.

Insight

Vision

I created a storyboard to illustrate the layout of the ADE program. The collaboration with the sales executive team allowed me to manage the copy that would be necessary in order for our sales associates to accomplish their goals.

Draft

I created a prototype to illustrate the design and layout of major ADE collateral, and included placeholders for any copy/media that was pending. This was a well received step before a final draft was submitted for approval.

Execution

After the approval of a final draft, I met with the sales team and our marketing director to develop this collateral into a brand package for the ADE program.
